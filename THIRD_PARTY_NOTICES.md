# Third-Party Notices

Repo2Prompts uses third-party packages installed through pnpm.

The machine-readable inventory is available at
[`docs/dependency-licenses.json`](docs/dependency-licenses.json). At the time of
this inventory, dependency licenses include:

- MIT
- Apache-2.0
- BSD-2-Clause and BSD-3-Clause
- ISC
- 0BSD
- BlueOak-1.0.0
- CC0-1.0 and CC-BY-4.0
- MPL-2.0
- LGPL-3.0-or-later
- Python-2.0

Packages under notice-bearing or reciprocal licenses require review before
shipping a public binary, container, or bundled distribution. In particular,
review the generated inventory for Apache-2.0 notices, MPL-2.0 files,
`@img/sharp-libvips-*` under LGPL-3.0-or-later, and `caniuse-lite` data under
CC-BY-4.0.

This summary does not replace the license text distributed with each installed
package.
